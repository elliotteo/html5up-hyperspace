Purpose

One of the issues with our animal model (see link in the header for model description) and HI models in general are that the same hypoxic-insult can give hugely varied physiogical responses depending on the individual physiology of the animal. Therefore it is essential that the outcomes of each experiment be understood and properly analysed, not just taken at face value. I wrote this markdown document to provide additional context to each individual experiment so any anomolies identified in the final analysis of the whole dataset could be quickly identified and understood.

The document had to serve 3 functions

  • Identify differences between individual piglets
  • Identify cause of death when necessary
  • Make recommendations to maximise the utility of future experiments

In a second project, I wanted to automate the data cleaning and extraction process which was previously done manually cell by cell. This process often took 4-5 hours to complete.

The second R Markdown attached was served 2 functions

  • Remove artefacts
  • To rapidly extract the extract the data of interest and save to CSVs for later analysis
